Free Tool

Know where you stand before you spend a rupee.

The Readiness Meter is a free 15-question self-assessment benchmarked against the NASSCOM AI Adoption Index. It takes 5 minutes and gives you an instant score across 9 pillars.

It is a starting point for self-reflection — a way to sense-check your instincts before a more substantive conversation. The actual diagnostic is a consultant-led exercise that goes significantly deeper.

Scores below 2.8 typically indicate The Diagnostic is the right starting point. Scores above 2.8 with a defined use case are usually ready for Full Lifecycle Implementation.
