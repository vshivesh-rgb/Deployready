Why enterprises need this

Three reasons AI stays stuck as a pilot — and never becomes a business outcome.

01
Decision paralysis

Organisations know AI needs to move into production. Large consultancies are too expensive. Internal teams don't know where to start. AI sits as a pilot forever — budget allocated, deadline missed, problem unchanged.

The result → perpetual pilot mode
02
Wasted training spend

Organisations invest in generic AI courses to upskill teams. Teams complete them. Awareness goes up. Deployment capability does not move. Budget is gone and the same pilots are still stalled six months later.

The result → awareness without capability
03
Post-consultancy collapse

Organisations hire consultancies to deploy AI. The project completes. Consultants leave. Nobody inside knows how to monitor, maintain, or build on it. Institutional knowledge walks out the door. Back to square one.

The result → dependency, not capability

About

Built by people who have watched AI fail inside real enterprises.

DeployReady was founded on a precise observation — not about AI's potential, but about where it breaks down. Enterprises spend on pilots. Most stall before production. The failure is almost never the model. It is the process, governance, and deployment layer that nobody owned, nobody trained for, and nobody was accountable for.

We built the training that addresses that layer specifically. Full lifecycle, India-specific, measurable, practitioner-led.
